Kirsty's Favourite Movies

My Top Tier List

Howl's Moving Castle

One of my favourite Studio Ghilbi movies, I really enjoyed the book also by Diana Wynne Jones. Very nostalgic of childhood.

Bullet Train

Fun and easy film set on a Shinkansen. I love the interplay of childishness and violence, it was very well thought out and very easy to watch.

Shrek

Great classic. Love the soundtrack and the twist on regular fairytale movies.
